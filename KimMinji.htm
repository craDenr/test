Kim Minji

  • Name: Kim Min-ji
  • Age: 20
  • Birthdate: May 7, 2004
  • Nationality: Korean

Minji (Hangul: 민지) is a South Korean idol under ADOR and the eldest member of the K-pop girl group, NewJeans. Minji, full name: Kim Min-ji, was born on May 7, 2004 in Chuncheon, Gangwon, South Korea. She is her family's middle child as she has an older brother who was born in 2003, and a younger sister born in 2011. During her elementary school years, Minji learnt English before moving to Canada for a few months to study abroad. At some point during her early teen years, Minji decided to become an idol trainee. She had many offers from agencies, including SM Entertainment, but she chose Source Music, becoming a trainee in 2017.
