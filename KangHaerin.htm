Kang Haerin

  • Name: Kang Hae-rin
  • Age: 18
  • Birthdate: May 15, 2006
  • Nationality: Korean

Haerin (Hangul: 해린) is a South Korean idol under ADOR and a member of the K-pop girl group, NewJeans. Haerin was born as Kang Hae-rin on May 15, 2006 in Seoul, South Korea. She attended school until her second year of middle school in 2020, during which she dropped out to become a trainee. On April 26, 2023, Haerin was announced as Dior's Newest Jewelry Global Ambassador & Fashion, Beauty House Ambassador.
