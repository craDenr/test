Lee Hyein

  • Name: Lee Hye-in
  • Age: 16
  • Birthdate: April 21, 2008
  • Nationality: Korean

Hyein (Hangul: 혜인) is a South Korean idol under ADOR and a member of the K-pop girl group, NewJeans. She is a former member of the kids group, USSO.GIRL. Hyein was born as Lee Hye-in on April 21, 2008, in Incheon, South Korea. She has an older sister, born in 2003, and an older brother, born in 2005. Hyein became a trainee of ADOR in 2021. The agency disclosed that she transferred from her previous international school to pursue homeschooling to finish her studies, stating that the choice was officially made by her parents. Hyein has been noted as being the "most experienced member" out of NewJeans, despite being the youngest of the group.
