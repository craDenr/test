Pham Hanni

  • Name: Phạm Ngọc Hân (Hanni Pham)
  • Age: 20
  • Birthdate: October 6, 2004
  • Nationality: Vietnamese-Australian

Hanni (Hangul: 하니) is a Vietnamese-Australian idol under ADOR and a member of the K-pop girl group, NewJeans. Hanni was born on October 6, 2004, in Melbourne, Victoria, Australia. Her father and mother are of Vietnamese descent.Her parents and grandparents migrated to Australia some time before her birth.She has a younger sister Jasmine,who was born in Melbourne in 2007.Hanni was raised as a Catholic. After moving to South Korea, she was enrolled in online courses at her former high school in Australia. In 2019, Hanni was part of an Australian K-pop dance cover group called AEMINA. In October 2019, she passed the Plus Global Auditions and became a trainee in 2020. She was later revealed to be the only member who passed from these auditions.
